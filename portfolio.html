Amazon

  • HC Staffing Project:

    • During my time at Amazon, I worked on the HC Staffing Project, which involved developing a strategic staffing model for a support department focused on quality assurance. The project involved analyzing workforce data and implementing innovative staffing solutions to optimize productivity, resulting in an estimated $1.2 million in annual savings. I used data analysis and modeling tools such as Excel, Python, and SQL to develop this solution. This project allowed me to further develop my skills in data analysis and modeling, and also gave me experience in working on large-scale business projects.

  • DPMO Tracker

    • The DPMO (Defects Per Million Opportunity) Tracker is a project I created during my time at Amazon. The project involved gathering information from different Amazon sites to track associate errors and specify those errors to better help associates overcome their barriers and improve their performance. I used Excel and VBA to build this tracker, which allowed me to easily analyze and manage data across different Amazon sites. This project allowed me to further develop my skills in data analysis and management, and also gave me experience in developing solutions for large-scale organizations.
